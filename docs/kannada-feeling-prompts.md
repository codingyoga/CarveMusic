# Kannada feeling prompts (reference)

Use this file when designing chips, onboarding, or copy-paste tests.

**Sendable prompt:** copy only the line under **Send** into CarveMusic chat (plain text, no smart quotes).  
**Feeling note:** short gloss for design only - not part of the chat message.

**Plain-text rules for prompts:** avoid curly quotes, em dashes, and fancy apostrophes so chips, logs, and search stay predictable.

---

## 1. Quiet evening

**Send:** Kannada slow acoustic melodies for a quiet evening  
**Feeling:** calm, unwinding, alone but soft.

---

## 2. Rainy night

**Send:** Kannada soft romantic melodies for a rainy night  
**Feeling:** cozy longing, gentle, intimate.

---

## 3. Nostalgic eras

**Send:** Kannada nostalgic 90s and 2000s film songs  
**Feeling:** memory lane, bittersweet warmth, familiar.

---

## 4. Low but gentle

**Send:** Kannada melancholy ballads when you are low but do not want noise  
**Feeling:** sad, held gently, not dramatic.

## 5. After a hard week

**Feeling:** tired to lighter, forward-looking, kind.

## 6. Frustrated, still melodic
